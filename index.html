Too Fluffy!

You will be given a random fluffy number at the start of the game.

There are four kittens below. By clicking on a kittens you will add a specific amount of fluffiness to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total fluffiness score goes above the random fluffy number. Too fluffy!

The fluffiness of each kitten is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the fluffiness of each kitten.
